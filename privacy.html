Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Better Nights and Bttr Nghts are used interchangeably to describe the app for which this privacy policy is enacted.

At Better Nights, our mission is to keep people safe and help people meet each other. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your phone number for SMS verification, as well as your name and profile details.
  • Location Data: We collect precise device location data (both in the foreground and background) to enable core features such as routing emergency alerts when the SOS function is used, and to verify attendance and matching within the Parties feature.
  • Usage Data: Basic interaction data to help us monitor app performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to operate the Better Nights app. This includes matching you with other users, facilitating events, and ensuring emergency contacts or services receive accurate coordinates if you activate an SOS.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data. Information is only shared with third-party cloud infrastructure providers necessary to host the app's databases, or with emergency services if an SOS is activated. All data is transmitted securely.

4. Data Retention

To ensure your account stays operational, Better Nights will generally retain your personal information until your account is deleted. However, Better Nights may retain certain information longer if necessary to provide our Service, defend our legitimate interests or those of third parties, comply with legal requirements, resolve or defend ourselves in disputes, investigate misuse or disruption of the Service, or perform agreements. We may also retain anonymous data indefinitely.

5. Underage Users

Better Nights is for users 18+. Users under the age of 18 are prohibited.

6. Links to Third-Party Websites

The Service may contain links to other websites. Better Nights is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other websites, including any personal or financial information collected by third-party payment processors. We encourage users to be aware of the privacy policies of other websites they visit. This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by Better Nights. Any time you click on a link (including advertising banners) or submit your personal information to a third-party website, you will be subject to that third party’s privacy policies.

7. Data Transfer

Better Nights is based in, and processes all data in, the United States. Better Nights is not to be used outside the borders of the United States and does not transfer data between the United States and any other country.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or permanently delete your account and associated data at any time directly through the app's settings.

9. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at bttrnghts@gmail.com.
