Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

At Bttr Nghts, our mission is to keep people safe and help people meet each other. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your phone number for SMS verification, as well as your name and profile details.
  • Location Data: We collect precise device location data (both in the foreground and background) to enable core features such as routing emergency alerts when the SOS function is used, and to verify attendance and matching within the Parties feature.
  • Usage Data: Basic interaction data to help us monitor app performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to operate the Bttr Nghts app. This includes matching you with other users, facilitating events, and ensuring emergency contacts or services receive accurate coordinates if you activate an SOS.

3. SMS Consent & Communications

By providing your phone number and clicking "Continue," you consent to receive a one-time verification SMS from Bttr Nghts. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt-out at any time by replying STOP. Your SMS consent and phone number are not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data. Information is only shared with third-party cloud infrastructure providers necessary to host the app's databases, or with emergency services if an SOS is activated. All data is transmitted securely.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or permanently delete your account and associated data at any time directly through the app's settings.
